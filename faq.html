Frequently Asked Questions

Voting Eligibility

Who can vote on initiatives?

Any member aged 16 or older can vote on community policies and initiatives.

How do I become a member?

Fill out the membership form. Membership is open to all, but voting requires being 16+.

Participation

Can I attend meetings?

Yes! All council meetings are open to the public.
