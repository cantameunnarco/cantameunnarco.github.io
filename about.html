Our Mission

The Lawrence Community Advocacy Coalition (LCAC) is committed to promoting environmental awareness, social justice, and educational equity. We provide a structured, youth-inclusive forum for community-driven advocacy and initiatives.

Who We Are

LCAC is a club dedicated to creating change through collaboration, education, and direct action. Our members work together to support policies and initiatives that lead to a more sustainable and inclusive community.

Leadership & Governance

LCAC is guided by an Executive Board, consisting of voting representatives, non-voting coordinators, advisors, and a chairperson who ensures the coalition’s mission is upheld.

To learn more about LCAC’s governance and structure, you can read our official Constitution.
