About the LCAC

Our Mission

LCAC provides a structured, youth-inclusive forum for community-driven advocacy focused on environmental protection, social equity, and accessible education.

Who We Are

We foster collaboration, education, and direct action to create meaningful change within our community.

Leadership & Governance

LCAC is guided by an Executive Board, consisting of representatives, non-voting coordinators, advisors, and a chairperson.
